ABOUT

Hi, I'm Sophia. I am a 2nd generation Canadian born Texan. I specialize in web design and development, brand identity, logo, poster design, packaging design, book design, and any other visual or graphic design elements related to branding. I have been living in Austin, Texas for about 17 years. My life experiences range from teaching special needs students in low income schools to being a visual artist, writer, poet, seamstress, musician, tech recruiter, triatholon finisher, server, and web developer.

As a former teacher, I‘ve developed strong skills in project management, communication, creative innovations, and the ability to excel when plans change. All of my vast experiences give me a unique set of skills that help me to be a skillful and intuitive designer. I have a passion for problem solving and I love creating designs that enhance lives. I truly believe that good design is changing the world.
