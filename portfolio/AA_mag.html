History of Asian American Magazine

Magazine Cover Design | Magazine Spread Layout Design

DETAILS

Asian Americans in the United States have been in fear in recent years but many people think that this hatred towards Asian Americans is new. This magazine talks about the long history of anti-asian sentiment and gives readers resources in order to educate themselves and others.

MY ROLE

The task of this project was to design the cover, back, and 9 inside spreads for a magazine with a total of 20 pages. The goal was to make sure there is a clear understanding of focal points, white space, balance, color, theory, and visual interest. I wanted to make sure that all type demonstrates an understanding of scale, readability, kerning, tracking, leading, style, and contrast. I also had to create the body type for this entire magazine which I pulled from many resources. I also pulled the photos from a variety of online resources to create this wonderful magazine.
