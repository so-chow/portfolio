Black Sheep Lodge

Logo | Business Cards | Menu | Coaster

DETAILS

Barton Springs Saloon is a low-key eclectic concerted gas station dive bar in the heart of the booming South Lamar neighborhood. The saloon lures locals with its low-key vibes as well as visitors with its jukebox, happy hour deals, great patio, and 32 beers on tap. They provide an authentic Texas experience and a place where everybody knows your name.

MY ROLE

For this project, I was tasked to redesign a local company in Austin, Texas. The redesign should include at least three brand elements. The design should effectively encapsulate the company's feel and essence and should communicate effectively the brand's message to the audience. I chose a nearby bar called Barton Springs Saloon. I felt that the current design could use a cleaner and more modern look. The goal was to revitalize the whole branding, identity, packaging, and logo in order to best represent this authentic bar. Our goal is to capture the personality and essence of the bar and put it on marketing materials to build a consistent message. We want to separate ourselves from the crowd and stand out in the best way possible. I was able to capture the grungy dive bar feel with the added texture on all brand assets. I used a clean western looking typeface that was more modern than the one they were already using. I also chose the colors black, white, and gold to capture the grungy feel in a sophisticated way. Overall, I believe this project was a success in elevating the logo, business cards, menu, and coaster to more so capture their Texas whole in the wall feel.
