Feeling Trees Book

Cover Design | Page & Spread Design

DETAILS

Feeling Trees is a book that I designed, illustrated, and wrote. It is about the process and the necessity of healing by doing the hard work of visiting past traumas. This is a passion project of mine and I drew all of the illustrations in Procreate on my iPad.

MY ROLE

In this project, I designed a square shaped book that is 12 pages long including the front and back cover. I used colors that invoke the feeligns of sadness, passion, and happines which are shades of teal and red. I also used a very clean serif typeface and a watercolor texture to give the covers a subtle character. All in all, I am very proud of this booklet and can't wait to print it!
