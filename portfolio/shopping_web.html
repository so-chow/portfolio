Shoe Shopping Website

Desktop

DETAILS

The objective of this project was to design an elegant website for a shoe company. This site should have the ability to choose the color, size, and amount to add to your bag. You should also be able to not only add an item to your bag but also to your favorites list. This website should list the name of the shoe, the price, payment options, at least 5 photos, materials, similar items, a place to add and read comments, and any other information that would be relevant. Lastly, the website should look aesthetically pleasing.
